# Classification Examples - Training Data

Real examples showing correct classification of customer statements.

## BUYLEAD EXAMPLES

### Example 1: buylead_availability
Customer: "Sir, I have not received any leads this week. My dashboard shows zero new leads."
CLASSIFICATION:
- Category: buylead_availability
- Severity: medium
- Reason: Customer reporting NO/LOW lead count

### Example 2: buylead_relevance
Customer: "I am getting leads but they are all wrong. I sell industrial pumps but leads are for water pumps domestic use."
CLASSIFICATION:
- Category: buylead_relevance
- Severity: high
- Reason: Customer receiving WRONG TYPE of leads

### Example 3: buylead_roi
Customer: "I have contacted 100 leads in last month but not a single order. All buyers say they already purchased from someone else."
CLASSIFICATION:
- Category: buylead_roi
- Severity: high
- Reason: Leads received but NOT CONVERTING to business

### Example 4: buylead_accessibility
Customer: "I can see the lead notification but when I open it, the phone number shows as 98XXXXXX45. How do I see full number?"
CLASSIFICATION:
- Category: buylead_accessibility
- Severity: medium
- Reason: Cannot ACCESS/VIEW buyer contact details

## DEACTIVATION EXAMPLES

### Example 5: Deactivation with BuyLead Issue
Customer: "Your service is useless. I paid 30,000 but not getting any relevant leads. I want to deactivate and get refund."
CLASSIFICATION:
- Category 1: buylead_relevance (severity: high)
- Category 2: deactivation (severity: critical)
- Category 3: payment (severity: high) - refund requested
- deactivation_intent: true
- Reason: Multiple issues - relevance complaint, deactivation request, refund demand

### Example 6: Deactivation Only
Customer: "Please cancel my membership. I don't want to continue."
CLASSIFICATION:
- Category: deactivation
- Severity: critical
- deactivation_intent: true
- Reason: Direct deactivation request without specific complaint

## PAYMENT EXAMPLES

### Example 9: Payment Dispute
Customer: "I paid yesterday but my plan is still showing expired. Amount has been deducted twice from my account."
CLASSIFICATION:
- Category: payment
- Severity: critical
- Reason: Double charge, payment not reflecting

### Example 10: Refund Request
Customer: "I want full refund. Your executive promised 100 leads per month but I got only 10."
CLASSIFICATION:
- Category 1: payment (severity: high) - refund
- Category 2: employee (severity: high) - mis-commitment
- Reason: Refund due to executive's false promise

## COMPLEX MULTI-ISSUE EXAMPLES

### Example 16: Multiple Issues
Customer: "Listen, I have many problems. First, I'm not getting leads. Second, when I do get leads they are wrong category. Third, my app is not working properly. And finally, I want to close my account because of all this."
CLASSIFICATION:
- Category 1: buylead_availability (severity: medium)
- Category 2: buylead_relevance (severity: high)
- Category 3: technical (severity: medium)
- Category 4: deactivation (severity: critical)
- deactivation_intent: true
- Reason: Multiple distinct issues must be captured separately

### Example 17: Root Cause Analysis
Customer: "I am not getting business from IndiaMART."
FOLLOW-UP NEEDED - This could be:
- buylead_availability (if no leads)
- buylead_relevance (if wrong leads)
- buylead_roi (if leads not converting)
