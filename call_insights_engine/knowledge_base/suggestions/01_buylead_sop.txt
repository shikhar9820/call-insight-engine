# BuyLead SOP - Standard Operating Procedure
# 80% of customer issues are related to buylead consumption

## Overview
The purpose of this SOP is to cover all the issues that a seller (customer/client for IndiaMART) might face while consuming buyleads. The SOP lays down steps of what a customer servicing executive should ideally discuss and do to resolve the seller's issues.

## Issue 1: BuyLead Page Accessibility
Client not able to access Buylead Page/Screen

### Executive Action
- Ask the seller to log in to the Seller Panel (desktop) or IndiaMART mobile app
- Guide the seller to the BuyLeads screen

### Resolution Flow
IF BuyLeads screen/page is not accessible or shows "page not loading / error":
- Diagnosis: Technical error
- Action:
  - Ask the seller to log out and log in again
  - Try an alternate browser (Chrome/Firefox)
  - On mobile, reinstall or update the app
  - If still persists: Assure customer of getting it checked with technical support
ELSE BuyLead page is accessible:
- Communicate the same to the client

## Issue 2: BuyLead Availability
Client does not see any leads on Seller Panel
Scenario: "No BuyLeads visible" - Seller can open BuyLeads page but sees blue band message: "You have viewed all relevant BuyLeads. Explore other leads you may like."

### Resolution Flow
IF Total BuyLeads approved in seller's categories are less than 10:
- Inform seller: "Currently, there are very few BuyLeads available in your listed categories"
- Explain that current demand in those categories is low
- Ask if seller deals in more products or items

  IF Seller says Yes (more items):
  - Go to Products section > Recommended Products
  - For each product in "Recommended Products":
    - Ask if seller deals in that product
    - Add those products to seller's catalog
    - Ensure minimum CQS (Catalog Quality Score) of 80%
    - Ensure: Product photo(s) uploaded, Description filled, Price added, ISQs filled
  - Cross-check catalog that changes are reflected
  - Ask seller to check BuyLeads next day

  ELSE IF Seller says No (no more categories):
  - Discuss each existing category one by one
  - Understand what products seller actually deals in
  - Cross-check if category mapping is correct
  - If incorrect: remap product to correct category
  - Find additional related categories based on product similarity

ELSE IF Total BuyLeads approved are decent numbers (>10-20):
  IF Category is High Sold MCAT:
  - Inform client BLs get consumed quickly in this category
  - Suggest consuming BLs faster (some categories get bought in 2 minutes)

  ELSE:
  - Check filters applied by client
  - If filters have narrowed search, suggest expanding: location, order value, category/product, lead type
  - Check previously consumed buyleads
  - If high consumption previously, ask about changes to catalog or filters

### Additional Parallel Check
- Verify calls and enquiries inflow
- Sellers might not get BuyLeads but may be getting business through calls or enquiries
- Discuss seller's response rate on calls and enquiries
- Educate: Quick response increases visibility

## Issue 3: BuyLead Relevance
Seller has available BuyLeads but says the BLs are not relevant

### Step 3.1: Check whether BLs are relevant
- Discuss each visible BuyLead with seller
- Ask: "Is this product something you actually deal in?"

IF product is relevant:
- Check past consumption
- Educate seller to consume more BLs quickly
- Ensure filters applied are correct
- Ask customer to mark relevant Buyleads as 'Interested'

IF product is not relevant:
- Ask customer to mark those BLs as BL NI ('Not Interested') - improves system matchmaking
- Ask customer to search for relevant BLs and mark them as 'Interested'
- Proceed to steps 3.2, 3.3, 3.4

### Step 3.2: Check Product/Category Mapping (MOST IMPORTANT)
IF Product is mapped under wrong category:
- Ask seller or offer to remap product to correct category
- Save changes and confirm reflected in catalog

IF Product falls under category for which BL consumed but not part of profile:
- Advise seller to either add that category under profile OR add under "Not Preferred Category"

IF Seller does not deal in that product at all:
- Remove or inactivate that product from seller's catalog

IF Very few BuyLeads visible across all categories:
- Recommend expanding product portfolio
- Go to Seller Panel > Products > Recommended Products
- For each suggestion: Ask if seller deals in it, if yes add it
- Cross-check updates appear in catalog
- Ask seller to revisit BuyLeads next day

### Step 3.3: Check Location Mapping
IF Location mismatches found between Preferred Location vs BuyLead posted location:
- Correct or update Preferred Locations list
- Explain expanding geography increases lead flow

IF Seller agrees to expand location coverage:
- Expand from: Hyperlocal > Local > City > State > Other Cities > Countries
- Save updated preferences

IF Seller restricts some areas:
- Add those to "Not Preferred Locations"

IF Seller refuses expansion:
- Respect preference but educate that limited geography = limited BuyLeads

### Step 3.4: Check ISQs, Order Value, and Quantity Filters
For complaints about "retail," "low MOQ," or "non-bulk" BuyLeads:

IF Seller does not accept low-quantity/retail leads:
- Guide to mark such leads as "Irrelevant BuyLeads"
- Show how to use filters: Order Value filter, Lead Type filter (Business/Bulk/GST)

IF Seller wants only bulk leads:
- Demonstrate filtering: Apply "Order Value" and "Lead Type = Bulk"
- View filtered results
- Show where previous Bulk BuyLeads originated

Check Pricing Relevance:
- Go to Seller Panel > Incorrect Price tab
- Review system suggestions
- If price too high or low: Advise correction to align with market standards

IF After all checks no relevant BuyLeads remain:
- Re-evaluate product range + location to widen match scope

## Issue 4: Poor ROI/Maturity
Seller complaints:
- Getting leads but not getting maturity/ROI
- Getting leads but buyer does not pick up call
- Getting leads but buyer picks call and denies requirement
- Getting leads but buyer discusses requirement then does not reply
- Getting leads but buyer discusses requirement but does not close deal

### Resolution Flow
IF Product/Category of consumed BL is not relevant:
- Refer to Steps 3.2 onwards

IF Product/Category of consumed BL is relevant:
- Check if buyer is not connecting or not agreeing to requirement

IF Unable to connect with Buyer:
- Verify if seller actually clicked "Contact Buyer Now"
- Ask about result of buyer contact attempt

IF Buyer connects but does not close deal:
- Educate: Not all BLs are converted
- Ask to consume more BLs at quick pace

IF Poor ROI despite deals with Buyer:
- Educate: BLs are both retail and bulk
- Focus on Average Order Value
- Consume more bulk BLs

### Additional Resolution Steps
IF Buyer's phone unreachable/calls unanswered:
- Ask seller to retry during different time slots (noon/evening)
- Suggest alternate reach-outs via WhatsApp or email

IF Buyer wanted different product/category mismatch:
- Correct mapping or raise buylead correction ticket

IF Price mismatch between seller quote and buyer expectation:
- Verify if seller pricing is realistic
- Ask seller to update price to competitive level

## Optional Steps

### Optional Step 1: Check Past Leads and Seller Activity
- Check type of leads received previously
- Check if leads were marked "Sold Out"
- If seller claims none received: Mention past leads that were sold out before consumption
- Educate seller:
  - Be active and consume BuyLeads within 0-4 hours of posting
  - Enable all Notification Settings (email, SMS, push)

### Optional Step 2: Seller Education
Important points to explain:
- BuyLeads are AI-distributed based on category and catalog quality
- Package tier does NOT influence BuyLead quality or volume
- Higher packages only improve product listing visibility, not lead count

Emphasize importance of:
- Active engagement (checking BuyLeads daily)
- Responding to enquiries and calls
- Updating product catalog frequently
- Marking irrelevant leads regularly
- Using negative marking in BuyLead preferences to exclude unwanted regions/categories

### Optional Step 3: Follow-Up and Closure
- Ask seller to recheck BuyLeads next day
- Assure customer of follow-up
