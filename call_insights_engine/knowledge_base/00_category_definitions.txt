# Issue Category Definitions - Classification Guide

This document provides clear definitions for each issue category to ensure accurate classification of customer service calls.

## BUYLEAD CATEGORIES

### buylead_accessibility
**Definition**: Issues where the customer cannot ACCESS or VIEW BuyLead details.
**Keywords**: cannot see, not visible, masked details, verification required, login issue, OTP issue
**Examples**:
- Customer cannot view buyer contact details (shows as 7XXXXXXX2)
- Primary/secondary number not verified
- Customer unable to login to seller panel
- OTP not received for verification

### buylead_availability
**Definition**: Issues related to LOW NUMBER or NO BuyLeads being available in customer's category.
**Keywords**: no leads, few leads, less leads, not getting leads, lead count low
**Examples**:
- "I am not getting any leads"
- "Only 2-3 leads per week"
- "No leads in my category"
- Low demand in product category

### buylead_relevance
**Definition**: Issues where BuyLeads received are NOT RELEVANT to customer's products/services.
**Keywords**: irrelevant, wrong category, not matching, different products, mapping issue
**Examples**:
- "I sell furniture but getting leads for electronics"
- "Buyers are from different industry"
- "Lead requirements don't match my products"
- Category mapping incorrect

### buylead_roi
**Definition**: Issues related to CONVERSION/RETURN from BuyLeads - leads not converting to business.
**Keywords**: no conversion, no response, waste money, not getting business, no orders, buyers not buying
**Examples**:
- "I contacted 50 leads but no orders"
- "Buyers are not responding"
- "I'm paying but not getting any business"
- "Leads are fake/not genuine"
- Buyer denied requirement

## SERVICE CATEGORIES

### payment
**Definition**: Issues related to PAYMENT transactions, billing, refunds, EMI.
**Keywords**: payment failed, double charged, refund, EMI, invoice, receipt, transaction
**Examples**:
- Double payment charged
- Refund not received
- EMI not working
- Invoice required
- Payment dispute

### subscription
**Definition**: Issues related to SUBSCRIPTION/MEMBERSHIP plans - upgrade, downgrade, renewal.
**Keywords**: plan, package, upgrade, downgrade, renewal, expiry, membership, benefits
**Examples**:
- Want to upgrade plan
- Plan benefits not clear
- Renewal pricing issue
- Package comparison query
- Auto-renewal setting

### pns (Preferred Number Service)
**Definition**: Issues related to INCOMING CALLS from buyers via IndiaMART's PNS system.
**Keywords**: PNS, calls, missed call, spam calls, call timing, number mapping, TrueCaller
**Examples**:
- Not receiving PNS calls
- Getting spam/irrelevant calls
- Calls at odd hours
- Missed call alerts not working
- Want to add/remove number from PNS

### catalog
**Definition**: Issues related to PRODUCT CATALOG - visibility, listing, images, descriptions.
**Keywords**: catalog, product, listing, image, description, visibility, search ranking, CQS
**Examples**:
- Products not visible in search
- Image upload issues
- Product description editing
- Catalog quality score low
- Product mapping issues

### enquiry
**Definition**: Issues related to DIRECT ENQUIRIES (not BuyLeads) from buyers.
**Keywords**: enquiry, direct message, buyer query, quotation, LMS
**Examples**:
- Not receiving enquiries
- How to respond to enquiry
- Enquiry notification issues
- LMS (Lead Management System) issues

### technical
**Definition**: Technical issues with WEBSITE/APP functionality.
**Keywords**: app, website, error, bug, loading, crash, update, browser, cache
**Examples**:
- App not working
- Website loading slow
- Error message appearing
- Need to reinstall app
- Browser compatibility issue

### employee
**Definition**: Complaints about IndiaMART EMPLOYEES - account managers, support staff.
**Keywords**: executive, manager, rude, misbehavior, not responding, mis-commitment, follow-up
**Examples**:
- Account manager not responding
- Employee made wrong promise
- Want to change account manager
- Excessive sales calls
- Rude behavior from staff

### deactivation
**Definition**: Customer wants to DEACTIVATE/CANCEL their IndiaMART subscription.
**Keywords**: deactivate, cancel, close, stop, terminate, refund, leave IndiaMART
**Examples**:
- "I want to deactivate my account"
- "Cancel my subscription"
- "I don't want to continue"
- Business closed
- Not satisfied with service
**SEVERITY**: Usually HIGH or CRITICAL - requires retention effort

### other
**Definition**: Issues that don't fit into any above category.
**Keywords**: general query, information, feedback, suggestion
**Examples**:
- General information query
- Feedback about service
- Suggestions for improvement
- Queries not related to above categories

## SEVERITY CLASSIFICATION

### critical
- Deactivation confirmed or imminent
- Legal threat mentioned
- Refund demanded with aggression
- Multiple unresolved issues over extended period

### high
- Deactivation intent expressed
- Escalation threatened
- Payment dispute active
- Significant financial impact mentioned

### medium
- Issue causing business impact
- Customer frustrated but willing to wait
- Requires follow-up action

### low
- Information query
- Minor issue easily resolved
- First-time mention of concern

## CLASSIFICATION PRIORITY

When multiple categories apply, prioritize:
1. **deactivation** - Always flag if customer mentions leaving
2. **payment** - Financial issues need immediate attention
3. **buylead_roi** - Business impact concerns
4. **buylead_relevance** - Quality of service issue
5. **technical** - If blocking normal operations
6. **Other categories** - Based on customer emphasis

## IMPORTANT NOTES

1. A single call can have MULTIPLE issues across different categories
2. Look for ROOT CAUSE, not just surface complaint
3. Customer saying "not getting business" could be:
   - buylead_availability (no leads)
   - buylead_relevance (wrong leads)
   - buylead_roi (leads not converting)
4. Always check for DEACTIVATION INTENT even if not the main topic
5. Note WHO mentioned the issue - customer complaint vs executive offering solution
