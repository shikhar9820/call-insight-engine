# IndiaMART Products and Packages

## BuyLead Allocation Policy

### Daily BuyLeads
- Valid only for the same day
- Automatically lapse at 11:59 PM every night
- Cannot be accessed or reactivated after lapse
- Advice: Consume leads as soon as they appear for maximum relevance

### Weekly BuyLeads
- Allocated on Sundays @ 12:00 AM
- Unused Weekly BuyLeads get lapsed on Saturdays @ 11:59 PM

## Product Packages and BuyLead Allocation

Path: Seller Panel >> Buyleads >> Balance BL (on right side of screen)
- Login to seller panel
- Click on buyleads section
- Right side of screen shows BL balance

### In Mobile App
Path: Open App >> Buyleads >> Upper right corner (three dots) >> Buyleads Balance
- Login to app
- Click on buyleads section (left)
- Top right corner click three dots
- Click on BL balance

## Sold Out Leads
When a lead shows as "Sold Out":
- The BuyLead has been consumed by maximum permissible sellers already
- Guide client to be highly active on panel/app
- Check if notification alert is switched on
- Verify contact information is correct
- Suggest enabling phone notification alert with distinctive ringtone
- Path for notifications: Seller Panel >> Setting >> Notifications Setting >> On all relevant alerts
- Guide client to consume other similar leads
- Suggest adding Auto Refresher extension to browser
- Show Past BL section to emphasize huge availability and importance of being active

## Enquiries

### Less Enquiries - Resolution Steps

1. Improve Product Catalogue
   - Add more relevant products & categories
   - Ensure proper product mapping (category + keyword)
   - Add prices to products (products with price get more visibility)
   - Upload clear photos
   - Add detailed specs: size, model, usage, brand, etc.

2. Increase Catalogue Quality Score
   - Target: Product Quality Score above 80
   - Higher score = better ranking and more enquiries
   - Path: Seller Panel > Products > Check Product Scores
   - Improve: image, video, description, specifications

3. Stay Active
   - Log in regularly (desktop/app)
   - Consume available leads quickly
   - Keep notifications ON
   - Don't miss out on calls
   - Ask for review and rating after every deal
   - Add more details on company profile for authenticity

### Less Conversion - Resolution Steps

1. Respond Immediately
   - Call within 2 minutes of lead consumption
   - Send quotations on WhatsApp/email immediately
   - First 2 sellers usually get buyer reply

2. Send Complete Quotations
   - Mention delivery & payment terms clearly
   - Include images & specifications in quotation
   - Keep quotation simple and clear

3. Regular Follow-Up
   - Call again in 6 hours if no reply
   - Message politely on WhatsApp/email
   - Follow-up increases conversion

4. Keep Pricing Competitive
   - Optimize pricing according to market

5. Use Lead Feedback Tools
   - Mark irrelevant leads as 'Not Relevant'
   - System provides better leads in future
   - Path: Seller Panel > Buyleads > Top right Mark as Not Relevant (BLNI)

6. Use LMS
   - Use filters and tags to sort enquiry data
   - Active follow-up on sorted data

## Products Management

### Add Products
Desktop:
- Go to "Products" section on left side of dashboard
- Enter relevant product details: name, description, price, features
- Upload clear, high-quality images
- Add product video if available
- Click "Finish" to add product

App:
- Click on Add Product button (bottom left)
- Fill in required details

### Bulk Product Upload
Desktop: Products >> More options (right side) >> Add multiple products
- Download file format, fill it, upload
App: Add products >> Add Multiple Products

### Pricing Guidelines
- Add prices to all products
- Competitive pricing helps with:
  - Instant visibility and more credibility
  - Relevant enquiries from relevant buyers
  - Quick buying decisions and better maturity chances
  - Preference in IndiaMART Search
- Bulk price update: Products >> More Options >> Quick Price Updates

### Image Guidelines

Mobile App:
- Minimum resolution: 500px
- Maximum dimensions: 2000px
- Maximum file size: 5MB without compression
- Product must cover entire image area (white background)
- No additional objects or mix of different products
- No text: watermark, logo, prices, offers, timestamps
- Different angles = separate images
- Each image must be unique

Seller Panel:
- Minimum resolution: 500px
- Preferred dimensions: up to 2000px (larger images resized from backend)
- Maximum file size: 10MB
- Product must cover 60-70% of image area (white background)
- No additional objects or mix of products
- No text: watermark, logo, prices, offers, timestamps
- No drawings or illustrations
- Each image must be unique

### Products Not Reflecting on Catalog
If product not listed, check if listing approved or rejected:
- Path: Seller.IM >> Manage products >> 'Inactive' tab >> 'Rejected' tab
- Under 'Inactive' tab see product and rejection reason
- Check self-help videos (middle right blue tab in products section)
- Contact: 9696969696 or customercare@indiamart.com

### How to Improve Catalog Quality

1. Check Product Score
   - Seller Panel > Product List > Check Product Quality Score
   - Identify products with score below 100

2. Enhance Each Attribute
   - Photo: Clear, high-resolution images (front, back, side view)
   - Video: Short product demo or factory video
   - Specifications: Size, material, model, capacity, brand, usage
   - Price & MOQ: Correct price range and minimum order quantity

3. Benefits of High Quality Catalog
   - Builds buyer trust
   - Increases enquiry flow and conversion chances
   - Better ranking in IndiaMART search results
