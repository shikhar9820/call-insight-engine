# IndiaMART Glossary and Business Terms

## Core Business Concepts

### BuyLead (BL)
- Definition: A buyer inquiry distributed to relevant sellers on IndiaMART
- Purpose: Connect sellers with potential buyers looking for their products
- Types: Daily BuyLeads, Weekly BuyLeads, Domestic, Foreign/Export
- Key Metric: BuyLeads are the primary value proposition for paid sellers

### Seller
- Definition: IndiaMART's paying customer who subscribes to packages
- Also called: Client, Customer, Supplier
- Goal: Get business leads and convert them to sales

### Buyer
- Definition: Person/business posting requirements on IndiaMART
- They search for products and post buy requirements
- Sellers consume BuyLeads to connect with buyers

### Maturity
- Definition: Actual business/deals/ROI generated from leads
- Indicates successful conversion of leads to sales
- Key metric for seller satisfaction

### Enquiry
- Definition: Direct inquiry from buyer to seller
- Different from BuyLead: Buyer directly contacts specific seller
- Can come through: Portal, calls, emails

## Product Packages

### MDC (Mini Dynamic Catalogue)
- Entry-level package
- 7 (monthly) or 10 (yearly) weekly BLs + 1 daily bonus
- Basic catalog listing and PNS

### TrustSEAL
- Trust-focused package
- 20 weekly BLs + 2 daily bonus
- TrustSEAL badge for credibility

### Maximiser
- Enhanced package
- 30 weekly BLs + 2 daily bonus
- Personal domain, corporate emails

### Star
- Multi-category package
- 30 base + 7 per category weekly BLs
- 2 base + 1 per category daily BLs

### Leader
- Premium package
- 40 base + 14 per category weekly BLs
- 2 base + 1 per category daily BLs

## Services and Features

### PNS (Preferred Number Service)
- Virtual phone number displayed on seller's catalog
- Forwards buyer calls to seller's mapped numbers
- Features: Office/Non-office hours, multiple number mapping, spam blocking (#1)

### LMS (Lead Management System)
- Tool to manage and track all leads
- Features: Search contacts, send replies, create quotations, reminders, labels
- Path: Seller Panel >> Lead Manager

### CLM (Customer Lifecycle Management)
- System for tracking customer interactions
- Used by executives to record call notes and follow-ups

### CQS (Catalog Quality Score)
- Score indicating quality of product listings
- Target: 80% or above
- Factors: Images, descriptions, prices, specifications
- Higher CQS = Better visibility and more leads

## BuyLead Related Terms

### BLNI (BuyLead Not Interested)
- Marking a lead as irrelevant/not interested
- Helps system improve future lead recommendations
- Path: Mark as "Not Relevant" on BL page

### Sold Out Lead
- Lead already consumed by maximum permissible sellers
- Indicates high competition in category
- Solution: Be more active, enable notifications

### Daily Bonus BuyLeads
- Extra leads allocated daily
- Expire at 11:59 PM same day
- Cannot be carried forward

### Weekly BuyLeads
- Main BL allocation
- Allocated Sunday 12:00 AM
- Expire Saturday 11:59 PM

### High Sold MCAT
- Categories where leads get consumed very quickly
- Some categories have leads sold in 2 minutes
- Requires high activity from sellers

## Category and Mapping Terms

### MCAT
- Product category on IndiaMART
- Proper mapping ensures relevant lead flow

### A-Rank Category
- High-performing category with good lead flow
- Priority category for the seller

### D-Rank Category
- Low-performing category
- May need review or removal

### PMCAT (Primary Mapped Category)
- Main category where product is listed
- Critical for lead relevance

### Negative (-VE) Category
- Categories marked to be avoided
- Helps exclude unwanted leads

### Category Mapping
- Associating products with correct categories
- Wrong mapping = irrelevant leads
- Path: Products >> Category Report

## Location Terms

### DLP (Domestic Location Preference)
- Geographic areas seller prefers for leads
- Can be: Hyperlocal, Local, City, State, National

### Preferred Location
- Areas where seller wants to receive leads
- Can expand to increase lead flow

### Non-Preferred Location
- Areas seller does NOT want leads from
- Helps filter out unwanted geographic areas

## Quality and Performance Terms

### ISQ (Important Selling Qualifiers)
- Key product attributes that help match leads
- Includes: Quantity, Order Value, Lead Type
- Filling ISQs improves lead relevance

### MOQ (Minimum Order Quantity)
- Minimum quantity seller accepts for orders
- Important for filtering retail vs bulk leads

### Lead Type
- Classification: Retail, Bulk, Business, GST
- Sellers can filter based on preference

### Order Value
- Expected value of the order/requirement
- Can filter leads based on minimum order value

## Escalation Levels

### L1 (Level 1)
- First level support
- Customer Care Executive
- Handles initial queries and basic troubleshooting

### L2 (Level 2)
- Second level support
- Senior Executive
- Handles complex issues, retention calls

### L3 (Level 3)
- Third level support
- Manager level
- Final escalation before deactivation approval

### FSF (Field Sales Force)
- On-ground sales team
- Can arrange meetings with sellers

### BM (Business Manager)
- Higher management
- Involved in retention and deactivation approval

### ZM (Zonal Manager)
- Zonal authority
- Final approval for deactivations and refunds

## Ticket and Issue Terms

### Deactivation
- Request to cancel/stop services
- Goes through retention process
- TAT: Up to 7 days with retention attempts

### Refund
- Return of payment after deactivation
- TAT: 7-10 working days after JV creation
- Requires ZM approval

### IVE
- International/Export related services
- Separate deactivation process

### JV (Journal Voucher)
- Financial document for refund processing
- Created after service download

### RCA (Root Cause Analysis)
- Analysis of why issue/deactivation occurred
- Required for deactivation approval

### Mis-commitment
- False promise or incorrect information given by executive
- Valid reason for refund/deactivation

## Activity Metrics

### DAU (Daily Active Usage)
- Daily activity level of seller
- Indicates engagement with platform

### BL Active Days
- Days seller actively consumed BuyLeads
- Higher = better engagement

### LMS Active Days
- Days seller was active on Lead Management System
- Indicates follow-up activity

### Response Rate
- Percentage of calls/enquiries responded to
- Higher response rate = better visibility

## Technical Terms

### Seller Panel
- Web dashboard for sellers
- Access at: seller.indiamart.com

### GLID (Global ID)
- Unique identifier for each seller account

### GEM Portal
- Government e-Marketplace
- For government tenders

### TrueCaller
- Call identification app
- Can block IndiaMART calls (issue)
- Solution: Unblock or remove
