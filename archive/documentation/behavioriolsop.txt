
SOP: Seller Behaviour Understanding & Insight Generation
(Using Activity, Consumption & Geography Signals)
Objective
To evaluate seller engagement, intent, and ROI potential by correlating responsiveness, lead consumption behaviour, category performance, and geographic preferences, and to generate clear insights & recommendations.

1. Seller Responsiveness & Engagement Health
Metric: pns_defaulter_count
Definition:
 Sellers who are not responding to direct buyer calls are tagged as PNS Defaulters.
Behaviour Interpretation:
High pns_defaulter_count = Low responsiveness + Low intent


Indicates disengagement, poor follow-up discipline, or operational issues


Impact on Other Metrics:
Directly lowers ROI even if lead volume is high


Reduces effectiveness of fresh lead consumption (cons_0_4_hrs)


Weakens category performance (ba_rank_mcats / cc_rank_mcats)



2. Lead Consumption Quality (Freshness Signal)
Metric: cons_0_4_hrs
Definition:
 Number of leads consumed within 0–4 hours (fresh lead consumption).
Behaviour Interpretation:
High value = High seriousness + fast response culture


Indicates strong buyer follow-up and higher conversion probability


Impact on Other Metrics:
Improves ROI significantly


Increases category visibility and ranking


Validates preferred geographies (city/state/country)



3. Category Engagement & Visibility
Metric: ba_rank_mcats
Definition:
 Only one transaction in a category over the last 6 months.
Behaviour Interpretation:
Seller is technically present but not actively working on that category


Low buyer exposure and poor algorithmic visibility


**Metric: cc_rank_mcats
 Definition:
 Category/product added but zero lead consumption.
Behaviour Interpretation:
Category is dormant or irrelevant for seller


Either mis-added category or lack of intent


Interlinking Logic:
High cc_rank_mcats + Low cons_0_4_hrs → Category clutter issue


High ba_rank_mcats → Needs category pruning or activation



4. Geographic Willingness vs Reach
Metric: cnt_neg_cities
Definition:
 Cities marked as “not preferred” by the seller.
Behaviour Interpretation:
High value = Restricted market reach


Leads to reduced lead inflow


**Metric: pref_city, pref_state, pref_country
 Definition:
 Geographies where seller actively consumes leads.
Behaviour Interpretation:
High values = Wider market appetite + scalability


Strong correlation with consistent lead flow


Interlinking Logic:
High cnt_neg_cities + Low preferred geography → Over-restriction


High preferred geography + High cons_0_4_hrs → Healthy expansion mindset



5. Composite Seller Behaviour Patterns (AI Scoring Logic)
A. High-Potential Seller
Low pns_defaulter_count


High cons_0_4_hrs


Low cc_rank_mcats


High preferred geographies


Insight:
Seller is engaged, responsive, and ROI-positive. Recommend scaling categories or geography.

B. Dormant / At-Risk Seller
High pns_defaulter_count


Low cons_0_4_hrs


High cc_rank_mcats


High negative cities


Insight:
Seller requires immediate intervention, coaching, or corrective nudges.

C. Misconfigured Seller
Good responsiveness


Poor category consumption (ba_rank_mcats, cc_rank_mcats)


Limited geography


Insight:
Issue is configuration, not intent. Recommend category & geography optimization.
